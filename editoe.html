Making of a friend

By Jahnvi Sajiv Menon

Once upon a time, there were 3 little girls. They were best friends. They had a flying bed, it was able to fit all three of them. But one day, their moms said that they were going to move to the country side and their enemy Nesa was coming because her parents were in Pennsylnvania for 8 days. The 3 girls started to pack and the next day, they left. They reached the country side after an 8 hour journey. They ate supper and retired to their flying beds to sleep and one of them had to sleep with Nesa so they had to go on a flying journey with Nesa. Theirs moms kissed them goodnight and went to bed. Then the girls told Nesa about their flying adventures and Nesa replied I don't want to come. I am going to sleep on the floor in a really danvi-like voice. They commanded their beds to fly so their beds got their red, yellow, pink wings and they turned small to get out of the window and turned big when they got out again.Then they went above the clouds and they stated to fly somewhere. While the bed was flying, the 3 girls was just laying down on the bed. The bed started to dive so the girls held on for their lives. In the meanwhile, Nesa was wating for them to come back. Then the bed landed in an island. The girls looked down on the island and they saw a big fat tree and they tried to open the door but it wouldn't budge. The girls saw a and they decided to press it. The opened the door and they saw a big beautiful pathway. They stared at it for a minute and started to walk through. At last, they ended up in a beautiful forest. There are 3 trees with little necklaces. The 3 necklaces had a little amulet which were the colors of the beds. The girls knew that it was almost morning so they went to their beds and flew home. The next day, they eat breakfast, got ready and went out to explore the country side. While they were exploring, they found a mansion and and pretty little girl with red hair came out. She asked Who are you guys and why were you knocking in my door?. The girls replied I am Molly, she is Christine, and she is Jennifer. Molly asked Who are you?. The girl replied My father is the mayor and I am Fiona. Jennifer asked if they wanted to be friends and Fiona agreed. Fiona asked Where is your home?. Christine found a playground and all 4 of them went. They played for hours. Then it became suppertime so they went home and they said I can't wait for my next adventure.

Shape drawer

Open the console in dev tools and type rect1.draw() or
cir1.draw(). Then you can create a new rectangle like this:
var VariableName = new rectangle(height,width,background color).
Height and Width must be numbers and background color
must be put into quotes like this: background-color. If you want to create a new
circle, then type: var VariableName = new circle(height,width,background color).
Same rules apply for the circle.
